This is definetly my favorite winter recipe and comes straight from the French Alps. It's full of cheese, cream, lardon and potatoes. I bet you will love it too!

The recipe is once again not that long to prepare, but probably anywhere from 1h to 1h30 with the cooking.

Bon appetit!

Ingredients

  • pepper
  • salt
  • oil
  • reblochon
  • onion
  • potatoes
  • lardon
  • garlic

Recipe

  1. Peel, cut and rince the potatoes
  2. Cook onions in oil until tender
  3. Add potatoes until golden on all sides
  4. Add lardon
  5. Cut reblochon in two
  6. press garlic everywhere on the oven dish
  7. Add half of the mix to the dish, then half of the reblochon then rest of the mix and the other half on top
  8. Cook for 20 minutes
  9. .... Voila!
